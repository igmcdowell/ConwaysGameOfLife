About:

"Conway's Game of Life" is a mathematical simulation; think of it as a sort of "Sim Bacteria" where cells live or die based on certain rules.

Instructions

Click and highlight squares to make them live/dead. You can also drag and drop existing patterns from the menu under the game grid.

Click "Advance" to run one day of reproduction, or "Run Simulation" to auto-advance.
